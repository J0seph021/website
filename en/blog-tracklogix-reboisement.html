    The app revolutionizing reforestation tracking

    Back to News
    Product January 22, 2026

    G.A. Logix is proud to announce the official launch of TrackLogix, an application specifically designed to revolutionize reforestation operations tracking. TrackLogix enables real-time planter tracking through connected smartwatches, automatically collects GPS data and generates instant production reports — all without any paperwork. A major advancement for reforestation contractors in Quebec and beyond.

    The problem: Costly and imprecise manual tracking

    Until now, tracking reforestation crews relied largely on manual methods. Foremen had to walk through planting blocks to count trees, estimate each planter's progress and compile production data by hand at the end of the day. This process was not only time-consuming, but also prone to inaccuracies that complicated billing, performance bonus management and reporting to contract holders.

    The solution: Connected smartwatches that collect everything automatically

    TrackLogix takes a radically different approach. Each planter wears a connected smartwatch that automatically records their GPS position throughout the day. The system detects the characteristic movements of planting and associates each GPS point with a planted tree. Data is transmitted in real time to the foreman's dashboard, providing a complete view of each team member's progress in the field. No manual entry is required from the planter — they simply wear the watch and do their work.

    Key features

    • Real-time dashboard: View the position and progress of each planter on an interactive map. Quickly identify completed areas and those remaining to be planted.
    • Automatic production reports: Daily, weekly and per-contract reports are generated automatically. No more hours of manual compilation at the end of the day.
    • Zero paperwork: All data is digital from the moment of collection. Timesheets, seedling counts and quality reports are produced without any paper forms.
    • Complete history: Every planted tree is geolocated and timestamped, creating a complete and verifiable record of reforestation operations.
    • Alerts and notifications: Receive alerts for zone departures, extended breaks or when daily production targets are met.

    How it works in the field

    In the morning, each planter puts on their connected smartwatch and starts their session with a single tap. The foreman checks their dashboard on a tablet or phone and sees the position of the entire crew in real time. Throughout the day, production statistics update automatically: number of trees planted, area covered, rate of progress. At the end of the day, the complete report is already ready — all that remains is to validate it and send it to the contract holder. What previously took two hours of administrative work is now done in just a few clicks.

    Availability and how to get started

    TrackLogix is ready for planting season for reforestation companies across Quebec. The application is compatible with Wear OS smartwatches and works with Android tablets and phones. Our team provides full deployment support: system configuration, crew training and technical support throughout the planting season. To learn more or to schedule a demonstration, contact our team today. Packages adapted to the size of your operation are available.
