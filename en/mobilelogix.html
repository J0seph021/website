    Your Mobile Field Companion

    MobileLogix is the Android mobile application designed specifically for forestry fieldwork. Built for equipment operators, truckers, mechanics, and foremen, it provides reliable and complete access to operational data directly in the field.

    Whether you have cellular coverage or are deep in the forest without a connection, MobileLogix lets you work efficiently with cloud synchronization and offline mode via Bluetooth.

    Features

    All the tools your teams need, right in their pocket

    Map and GPS

    Record your GPS tracks in the field, search for equipment by worksite, and choose your base maps including custom MbTiles. Display layers from WFS servers, configure GPS accuracy to your needs, and enjoy automatic zoom for smooth navigation.

    Drawing Tools

    Place markers, draw lines and polygons with automatic length and area calculations. Create work orders directly from the map and take georeferenced photos to document your field observations.

    Work Orders

    Create work orders for equipment breakdowns or maintenance. Track parts used, record hours and kilometres, and document oil changes and greasing. Everything is recorded and synchronized automatically.

    Timesheets

    Enter hours worked by worksite and equipment with automatically calculated totals. Record equipment hours and kilometres and complete inspection checklists directly from your mobile device.

    Production

    Record your daily production by species and quality. Enter volumes in m³ for harvesters and number of trips for forwarders. A weekly entry option is also available to simplify the process.

    Fuel Management

    Record fuel consumption for each fill-up. Track the transfer from the source tank to the destination equipment for precise control of your fuel costs on each worksite.

    Deliveries

    Create delivery slips with all the necessary shipping details. Take photos of transport tickets to keep a visual record of each delivery and simplify payment verification.

    Synchronization

    Enjoy real-time cloud synchronization when an internet connection is available. In areas without coverage, two-way Bluetooth synchronization between tablets and phones ensures operational continuity.

    Employee and Administration

    MobileLogix offers comprehensive administration features to efficiently manage your field teams.

    • Employee list management for administrators
    • Licence and login credential delivery
    • Licences saved in the cloud for easy recovery
    • Authentication via Google or Apple account
    • Trip lists by worksite
    • Real-time GPS accuracy display
    • Access to documents by worksite
    • System diagnostics screen for troubleshooting

    What's New in 2026

    The latest improvements to MobileLogix

    Built-in AI Assistant

    A forestry-specialized chatbot providing real-time responses with streaming and Markdown formatting for instant field assistance.

    Smart Voice Readout

    Assistant responses are read sentence by sentence, ideal for hands-free use in equipment cabs.

    Automatic Worksite Reports

    Generate professional HTML worksite reports directly from the AI chatbot to document your operations.

    Report Template Gallery

    Choose from a gallery of custom report templates to tailor documentation to your specific needs.

    Government WMTS Layers

    Access official orthophotos and cartographic layers from the Quebec government directly on your mobile device.

    Bluetooth SPP GPS

    Bluetooth SPP GPS support for increased accuracy under dense forest canopy where the built-in GPS is insufficient.

    USB Physical Buttons

    Connect USB physical buttons (CP2102) to allow cab operators to control the application without touching the screen.

    Hybrid CTS + Serial Mode

    Hybrid CTS and serial mode with LED GPS tracking indicator via RTS signal for visual connection status monitoring.

    Material Design 3 Interface

    A modernized user interface with Material Design 3 for a more intuitive and pleasant experience.

    Smoother Map

    Optimized orientation and smoothed compass for a more fluid and precise map navigation in the field.

    Improved Battery Life

    Power consumption optimizations to extend your device's battery life during long days in the forest.

    More Reliable Synchronization

    Smart automatic retry after connection loss to ensure your data is always up to date.

    Android 15+ and WearOS Support

    Improved compatibility with Android 15 and recent versions, along with enhanced WearOS support for smartwatches.

    Multi-Company Management

    Manage multiple companies from a single installation with no risk of data loss when switching contexts.
