    ForestLogix

    ForestLogix is our complete forest management solution. This software centralizes all operational data and enables managers to plan, monitor and analyze all forestry activities in one place. From contract management to detailed report generation, ForestLogix simplifies administrative processes and improves decision-making through real-time data.

    Key features include: worksite management, production tracking, volume calculation, advanced GPS mapping, subcontractor management and automatic generation of reports compliant with industry standards. Available on Windows desktop, Windows tablet, Android and iPhone.

    Discover ForestLogix

    MobileLogix

    MobileLogix is the mobile companion app for ForestLogix, specifically designed for field operations. Available on rugged tablets, it allows crews on the worksite to enter data, consult work plans and communicate with the office in real time, even in areas with limited network coverage.

    The application works in offline mode and automatically synchronizes data as soon as a connection becomes available. Its intuitive interface, integrated mapping and customizable forms make it an indispensable tool for forest work.

    Discover MobileLogix

    SyncLogix

    SyncLogix is our data synchronization solution for on-board computers from harvesters and other mechanized harvesting equipment. This module ensures reliable and automated transfer of production data between forest machines and the ForestLogix system.

    With SyncLogix, managers have access to harvesting data in near real time, enabling precise productivity tracking, operations optimization and a significant reduction in manual data entry errors.

    Discover SyncLogix

    TrackLogix

    TrackLogix is our silvicultural worker tracking solution. This application allows you to locate crews in the field, monitor the progress of silvicultural work and ensure worker safety in the forest through a precise geolocation system.

    Foremen and managers can view their crews' positions in real time, validate treated areas and generate detailed productivity reports. TrackLogix helps improve operational efficiency while strengthening workplace safety.

    Discover TrackLogix

    SIGLogix

    SIGLogix is our suite of extensions for geographic information systems (GIS). Available for ArcGIS Pro, QGIS and ArcMap, these plugins add specialized forestry tools directly into your existing GIS environment.

    Quick toolbar, offline maps for the field, web publishing, automatic MRNF compliance, culvert sizing, Garmin GPS export and harvest data analysis. Specifically designed for Quebec forestry with a bilingual interface.
