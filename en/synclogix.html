    Your Data, Always Up to Date

    SyncLogix ensures the seamless flow of all your data between field devices and your office systems. With real-time cloud synchronization and Bluetooth fallback mode for areas without connectivity, your teams always have the most up-to-date information.

    No more manually transferring files or re-entering data at the office. SyncLogix eliminates duplicates, data loss, and transmission delays for maximum operational efficiency.

    How It Works

    A simple four-step process to keep your data synchronized

    Step 1 — Field Collection

    Data is captured directly on your teams' tablets and phones: GPS tracks, timesheets, georeferenced photos, work orders, production, and deliveries. Everything is recorded locally on the device in real time.

    Step 2 — Cloud Synchronization

    As soon as an internet connection is available, data synchronizes automatically with the cloud server. No manual intervention is required. Data is transmitted securely and instantly.

    Step 3 — Offline Bluetooth Synchronization

    In areas without cellular coverage, two-way Bluetooth synchronization takes over. Equipment-mounted tablets exchange data with nearby operators' Android phones, ensuring continuous information flow.

    Step 4 — Available at the Office

    All collected data is instantly available on the ForestLogix desktop version. Generate your reports, analyze production, and manage your operations with data that is always up to date, with no delays or re-entry required.

    Key Features

    SyncLogix offers a comprehensive set of features to ensure the reliability and availability of your data at all times.

    • Real-time cloud synchronization across all platforms (desktop, tablet, Android, iPhone)
    • Two-way offline Bluetooth synchronization between tablets and Android phones
    • Automatic data sharing across all company devices
    • Wi-Fi synchronization coming soon (replacing the Bluetooth requirement)
    • Live WFS synchronization with ArcGIS Online for geospatial data

    Compatibility

    SyncLogix is integrated into the entire ForestLogix ecosystem and works seamlessly with all versions of the platform.

    • ForestLogix Desktop (Windows)
    • ForestLogix Tablet (Windows)
    • ForestLogix Android
    • ForestLogix iPhone

    On the hardware side, SyncLogix is compatible with Emdoor Windows tablets and Topicon Android tablets, offering proven reliability in demanding forestry field conditions.
