    How we protect your personal data

    Last updated: May 25, 2026

    G.A. Logix Logiciels inc. respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the personal information you entrust to us. This policy describes what data we collect on www.galogix.ca, how we use it, and your rights, in compliance with Quebec's Act respecting the protection of personal information in the private sector (Law 25).

    1. Data controller

    G.A. Logix Logiciels inc.
    100 Route 165 Sud, Plessisville (Quebec) G6L 2Y2, Canada
    Phone: 819-252-2000
    Email: info@galogix.ca

    2. Information we collect

    When you visit www.galogix.ca, we may automatically collect:

    • IP address (anonymized by Google Analytics)
    • Browser type and version, operating system
    • Pages visited, time spent, traffic source (referrer)
    • Approximate geographic region

    If you fill out a contact form, we collect the information you provide (name, email, phone, message).

    3. Cookies and similar technologies

    We use the following cookies:

    CookieProviderPurposeDuration
    _gaGoogleIdentifies a unique visitor2 years
    _ga_FW2DLMHSMNGoogleSession state2 years
    _gidGoogleIdentifies today's visitor24 hours

    These cookies are only set after your explicit consent via the banner that appears on your first visit. If you refuse, no Google Analytics cookies are placed.

    localStorage (local storage)

    We also use your browser's localStorage to remember your language preference (galogix-lang) and your consent choice (galogix-cookie-consent). This data:

    • Stays on your device and is never sent to a server
    • Is not a cookie in the technical sense
    • Is essential for the proper functioning of the site (user preferences)

    4. Purposes of processing

    • Measure audience and improve our site's content
    • Identify popular pages and user journeys
    • Remember your preferences (language, consent)
    • Respond to your contact requests

    5. Recipients and transfers outside Quebec

    Your browsing data is transmitted to Google LLC (United States) as part of the Google Analytics service. Google commits to respecting the EU–U.S. Data Privacy Framework and the similar framework for Canada. No data is sold to third parties.

    6. Retention period

    • Google Analytics cookies: duration indicated in the table above
    • Language and consent preferences: until you delete them
    • Contact requests: 24 months after the last exchange

    7. Your rights

    Under Law 25, you have the right to:

    • Access your personal information
    • Rectify inaccurate or incomplete information
    • Request deletion of your information
    • Withdraw your consent at any time
    • File a complaint with the Commission d'accès à l'information du Québec (cai.gouv.qc.ca)

    To exercise these rights, write to info@galogix.ca. We will respond within 30 days.

    8. Security

    The site is served over HTTPS (TLS encryption). We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data against loss, unauthorized access, or disclosure.
