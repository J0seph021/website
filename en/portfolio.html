    Our Partnerships

    Groupement forestier Québec (GFQ)

    G.A. Logix maintains a strategic partnership with the Groupement forestier Québec, a major player in Quebec's forestry industry. This collaboration allows us to stay at the forefront of the sector's needs and develop solutions that meet the requirements of a wide range of forestry companies across the province.

    The GFQ brings together forest owners and companies in the sector, offering a platform for collaboration and knowledge sharing. Our partnership with this organization gives us access to invaluable field expertise that directly fuels the development of our products and services.

    G.A. Logix is always open to new partnerships and collaborations. If your company is looking for technology solutions tailored to the forestry sector or if you would like to explore collaboration opportunities, do not hesitate to contact us.
