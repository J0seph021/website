    Reforestation Tracking, Reinvented

    TrackLogix is a new reforestation tracking application that transforms how forestry companies manage their planting operations. By tracking tree planters in real time using smartwatches, TrackLogix automatically collects GPS data and generates production reports instantly, with zero paperwork.

    No more manual logs, paper forms, or rough estimates. TrackLogix delivers unmatched precision and speed for tracking your reforestation crews.

    The Problem

    Manually tracking reforestation crews is a time-consuming and error-prone task. Foremen must travel long distances to check on work progress, and paper reports lead to delays, inaccuracies, and frequent data loss.

    Without reliable GPS data, it is difficult to validate planting quality, measure the actual coverage of reforested areas, and assess each planter's individual productivity. These shortcomings result in additional costs and inefficient operations management.

    The Solution

    TrackLogix eliminates the problems of manual tracking through technology

    Real-Time Tracking

    Track each planter's position in the field in real time using a smartwatch. Instantly see who is working where and monitor all your planting zones from your office or vehicle.

    Automatic GPS Collection

    GPS data is collected automatically throughout the day with no manual input from the planters. Every movement is recorded with precision, creating a complete history of covered areas.

    Instant Reports

    Production reports are generated instantly from the collected data. No paperwork, no re-entry. Data is available at the end of the workday for immediate analysis.

    Dashboard

    A real-time dashboard displays the activity of all planters, covered areas, and productivity indicators. Managers get a complete overview to make informed decisions quickly.

    Technical Features

    TrackLogix is designed to integrate with specialized hardware, offering maximum flexibility for your field operations.

    • CTS (input) — Button functionality for triggering field actions
    • RTS (output) — 5V signal when GPS tracking is active, 0V when stopped. Allows connection of an LED indicator for immediate visual feedback
    • Arduino Integration — Hidden buttons that appear automatically when an Arduino is detected, enabling custom hardware configurations
    • USB Configuration — USB configuration page with a clean display for simple and quick device setup
