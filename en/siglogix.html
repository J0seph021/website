    Professional forest geomatics

    SIGLogix is G.A. Logix's GIS (Geographic Information System) integration solution, designed for professional forest geomatics. Developed to work seamlessly with ArcMap, ArcGIS Pro and QGIS, SIGLogix enables geomatics specialists to perform advanced spatial analyses and produce high-quality maps for the forestry sector, regardless of their preferred GIS platform.

    SIGLogix bridges the gap between professional GIS tools and the ForestLogix platform, ensuring that cartographic data created in the office is faithfully reproduced on field devices with the correct symbology and information layers.

    Features

    Powerful GIS tools for your geomatics workflows

    ArcMap / ArcGIS Pro Integration

    Seamless integration with Esri products for advanced GIS workflows. Work in your familiar environment and publish your data directly to ForestLogix for field use.

    WFS Publishing

    Create and publish WFS layers from ArcGIS Pro and ArcGIS Online to ForestLogix for live synchronization. Your geospatial layers are automatically updated across all field devices.

    Automatic Symbology

    Support for .qml (QGIS), .lyr (ArcMap) and .lyrx (ArcGIS Pro) symbology files. Your maps maintain their professional appearance across all platforms, from the office to the field.

    Import / Export

    Shapefile import with automatic detection of associated symbology. GPX export for field data and GeoJSON format support for interoperability with other systems.

    MbTiles Creation

    Generate custom MbTiles maps from LiDAR data and orthophotos for offline field use. Your operators have access to detailed maps even without an internet connection.

    Map Distribution

    Transmit cartographic data to your contractors and subcontractors with the appropriate symbology. Each stakeholder receives the information layers relevant to their specific operations.

    WFS Workflow

    Publish your GIS layers to the field in five simple steps

    Step 1 — Layer Preparation

    Add the ga_chantier_id and ga_etiquette fields to your layers in ArcGIS Pro. These fields allow ForestLogix to automatically associate polygons with the correct worksites in the field.

    Step 2 — Publishing from ArcGIS Pro

    Share your layer as a Web Layer (Share as Web Layer) directly from ArcGIS Pro. The layer is published to your ArcGIS Online portal with all its properties.

    Step 3 — Configuration in ArcGIS Online

    Create a View Layer in ArcGIS Online and enable the WFS service. This view allows you to control data access while providing a standard connection point for ForestLogix.

    Step 4 — Connection in ForestLogix

    Connect the WFS service in ForestLogix's external WFS settings. The application automatically detects available layers and configures them for display on field devices.

    Step 5 — Automatic Field Display

    Polygons corresponding to each worksite are automatically displayed on operators' field devices. Layers are updated in real time via live WFS synchronization.

    Compatibility

    SIGLogix is designed to integrate with the most widely used GIS tools in the Quebec and Canadian forestry industry.

    • QGIS — Free and open-source GIS software, .qml symbology
    • ArcMap — Esri's classic desktop GIS application, .lyr symbology
    • ArcGIS Pro — Esri's next-generation desktop GIS application, .lyrx symbology
    • ArcGIS Online — Esri's cloud-based GIS platform, WFS publishing
    • Esri FieldMaps — Esri's mobile application for field data collection
